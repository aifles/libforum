Invitation

Dear Ladies and Gentlemen,

We invite you to participate in the international conference and seminar for the following topic:

"LIBRARY: TODAY, TOMORROW - IV" FORUM
"THE ACADEMIC LIBRARY DEVELOPMENT"

We are sending you the guidelines for the international conferences and seminars organized by the Library of the National University of Mongolia. You can give a presentation to the related topic of "The Academic Library Development" or just introduce your Library and share experiences. Please let us know your participation interest by the following deadline.

  • • If a visa is required, by May 15, 2025

  • • If a visa is not required, by June 15, 2025

After receiving your response, the official process will begin, and we will send the required documents and official invitation and etc.

Sincerely yours,

Dr. Tseren Ganbaatar

Dean of the library, National University of Mongolia.
